Meet And Greet

Meet and greet parking is also known as valet parking. It is the most convenient way to park your car at the airport without hassle. Meet and greet is a popular parking option, but it is mostly preferred by families, especially if you are travelling lots or luggage or children in company.

Meet and greet parking is the easiest way to manage your airport parking. It only requires you to drive to your airport terminal, hand over your car to the driver awaiting your arrival, and head for the departure lounge. When you return, you merely call the ‘operations number’ provided to you, and the driver will bring your car to the meet up point outside your terminal. It is simple and ideally designed for your convenience.
